# Continuation Roadmap After the 5-Day Book

## Week 1: AI study habits

- Build a prompt bank.
- Use active recall daily.
- Create one mistake notebook.
- Use AI to generate quizzes, not to avoid studying.

## Week 2: AI projects

- Build one presentation.
- Create one infographic.
- Make one AI-assisted study kit.
- Present your project to family or classmates.

## Week 3: Research and verification

- Learn how to compare sources.
- Practice checking dates and authors.
- Ask AI for assumptions and possible errors.
- Rewrite findings in your own words.

## Week 4: Future skills

- Try basic coding support.
- Practice speaking or viva with AI.
- Create a learning portfolio.
- Reflect on ethical AI use.

## Long-term goal

Become a student who can combine curiosity, discipline, communication, creativity, and responsible AI use.
