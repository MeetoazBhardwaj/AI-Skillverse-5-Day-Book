# Contributing to AI Skillverse Student Book

This repository is designed for student learning. Contributions should be:

- Safe for children and teenagers.
- Clear and age-appropriate.
- Accurate and verifiable.
- Ethical and aligned with school learning.
- Free from private student information.
- Written without emojis to maintain a professional book style.

## Suggested contribution types

- Better examples.
- More activities.
- Additional diagrams.
- Teacher facilitation notes.
- Safer prompts.
- Corrections to unclear explanations.
