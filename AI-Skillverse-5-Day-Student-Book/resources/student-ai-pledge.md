# Student AI Pledge

I understand that AI is a powerful learning tool.

I will use AI to:

- Understand concepts.
- Practice questions.
- Improve my writing.
- Organize my ideas.
- Create responsibly.
- Learn from mistakes.

I will not use AI to:

- Cheat.
- Copy homework dishonestly.
- Create fake information.
- Harm or insult others.
- Share private information.
- Avoid thinking.

I will remember:

```text
AI can help me learn faster, but I must still think, verify, practice, and act honestly.
```
