# AI Skillverse Prompt Library

Use these prompts responsibly. Replace text inside square brackets.

## Explanation prompts

```text
Act as a patient teacher. Explain [topic] to a Grade [grade] student. Use simple language, one real-life example, and three check questions.
```

```text
Explain [topic] in three levels: Grade 4 level, Grade 8 level, and Grade 12 level.
```

```text
Explain [topic] using a story, then summarize it in five bullet points.
```

## Quiz prompts

```text
Ask me 10 questions from [topic], one at a time. Wait for my answer. Score me at the end and explain my mistakes.
```

```text
Create a mixed quiz with easy, medium, and hard questions on [chapter]. Do not show answers until I try.
```

## Exam prompts

```text
I have an exam on [date]. I need to study [chapters]. I can study [time] per day. Create a realistic plan with revision, practice questions, and breaks.
```

```text
Act as a strict examiner. Evaluate my answer for [marks] marks. Tell me what marks I may lose and how to improve.
```

## Writing prompts

```text
Help me improve this paragraph. Keep my meaning, fix grammar, and explain the top three improvements.
```

```text
Give me an outline for an essay on [topic]. Do not write the full essay. Give points I can develop in my own words.
```

## Coding prompts

```text
I am getting this error: [paste error]. Explain what it means in simple language. Give me hints first, then the smallest correction.
```

```text
Review my code for logic mistakes. Do not rewrite the full code unless necessary. Explain each issue clearly.
```

## Presentation prompts

```text
Create a 7-slide outline on [topic] for Grade [grade]. Include slide title, key points, visual idea, and speaker notes.
```

```text
Help me convert this chapter into a classroom presentation. Include one activity and three audience questions.
```

## Verification prompts

```text
What parts of this answer should I verify from my textbook or teacher? List possible mistakes or assumptions.
```

```text
Give me a checklist to verify whether this information is accurate.
```

## Ethics prompts

```text
Am I using AI ethically for this task? Here is what I plan to do: [explain]. Tell me what is allowed, risky, or dishonest.
```
