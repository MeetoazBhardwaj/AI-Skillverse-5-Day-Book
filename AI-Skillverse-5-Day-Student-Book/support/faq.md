# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can AI do my homework?

AI can help you understand homework, but it should not replace your own effort. Ask for hints, explanations, and feedback.

## Is AI always correct?

No. AI can make mistakes and sometimes invent facts. Always verify important information.

## Can I upload my textbook or notes?

Only upload material that is allowed by your school, parent, or teacher. Do not upload private, paid, restricted, or personal material without permission.

## Which AI tool is best?

There is no single best tool for everything. Use the tool that matches the task. For explanations, chat assistants are useful. For research, use tools with sources. For presentations, use design tools. For notes, use source-grounded tools.

## Will AI replace teachers?

No. AI can support learning, but teachers understand students, classrooms, emotions, school expectations, and human growth in ways AI cannot.

## Can younger children use AI?

Yes, but with adult supervision. Younger students should focus on safe questions, stories, vocabulary, and quizzes.

## How do I know if I am cheating?

Ask: Did I understand it? Did I write it in my own words? Did I follow teacher rules? Would I be comfortable telling my teacher how I used AI?
