# Final Project Rubric

Total: 100 marks

| Criteria | Marks | What excellent work looks like |
|---|---:|---|
| Understanding of topic | 20 | Student can explain the topic clearly without reading directly from AI output. |
| Prompt quality | 15 | Prompts are specific, ethical, and learning-focused. |
| Verification | 15 | Facts are checked using textbook, teacher, or reliable sources. |
| Creativity | 15 | Project has original examples, visuals, questions, or design. |
| Ethical AI use | 15 | No private data shared, no copying, AI use is honest. |
| Presentation clarity | 10 | Work is organized, readable, and suitable for the audience. |
| Reflection | 10 | Student explains what AI helped with and what they did themselves. |

## Reflection questions for submission

1. What AI tool did you use?
2. What prompts did you use?
3. What did AI help you with?
4. What did you write, design, or decide yourself?
5. How did you verify the content?
6. What would you improve next time?
